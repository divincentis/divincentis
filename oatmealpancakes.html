Notes

  • The batter should be fairly thick but still pourable. If it seems too thick, add a little more milk, one tablespoon at a time.
  • Letting the batter rest is important - it allows the oats to soften and the flour to hydrate properly.
  • If using banana, the pancakes will be slightly sweeter and more moist.
  • These pancakes freeze well. Layer them between pieces of wax paper and store in a freezer bag for up to 2 months.
