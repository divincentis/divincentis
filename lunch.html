Christmas Eve Lunch Plan

Join us for a delicious Italian-themed lunch featuring a variety of appetizers, main courses, sides, and desserts. Below is the full menu, recipes, and a shopping list to help us prepare!

Menu

Appetizers

  • Antipasto Platter
  • Caprese Skewers

Main Courses

  • Traditional Lasagna
  • Vegetarian Lasagna
  • Chicken Marsala
  • Pasta Bar (Spaghetti and Penne)

Sides

  • Garlic Bread
  • Mixed Green Salad

Desserts

  • Tiramisu
  • Pizzelle Cookies
  • Vanilla Gelato with Sprinkles

Recipes

Appetizers

Antipasto Platter: Arrange cured meats, cheeses, marinated olives, artichokes, roasted red peppers, and sliced baguette on a platter.

Caprese Skewers: Assemble cherry tomatoes, mozzarella balls, and fresh basil leaves on toothpicks. Drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.

Main Courses

Lasagna: Layer cooked lasagna noodles, marinara sauce, ground beef or spinach, ricotta mixture (ricotta, eggs, parmesan), and mozzarella in a baking dish. Bake at 375°F for 45 minutes.

Chicken Marsala: Sauté floured chicken breasts in olive oil and butter, add mushrooms and garlic, deglaze with Marsala wine, and simmer with chicken broth until thickened.

Pasta Bar: Serve cooked spaghetti and penne with marinara, alfredo, or butter and cheese.

Sides

Garlic Bread: Spread garlic butter and parsley on baguette slices, sprinkle with parmesan, and bake at 400°F until golden.

Mixed Green Salad: Toss romaine, radicchio, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onions with Italian vinaigrette.

Desserts

Tiramisu: Layer coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone mixture, dust with cocoa, and refrigerate overnight.

Pizzelle Cookies: Cook batter in a pizzelle iron until golden and crisp.

Kids' Dessert: Top vanilla gelato with sprinkles and chocolate sauce.
