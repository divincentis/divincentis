Recipes

Classic Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • • 1 lb lasagna noodles
  • • 1 lb ground beef
  • • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • • 2 24oz jars marinara sauce
  • • 32 oz ricotta cheese
  • • 2 eggs
  • • 4 cups mozzarella, shredded
  • • 1 cup parmesan, grated
  • • 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. 1. Brown meats, drain
  2. 2. Mix ricotta, eggs, 1 cup parmesan, seasonings
  3. 3. Layer: sauce, noodles, meat, ricotta mix, mozzarella
  4. 4. Repeat layers 3 times
  5. 5. Cover with foil, bake 375°F for 25 mins, uncover and bake 25 more mins

Chicken Piccata

Ingredients:

  • • 6 chicken breasts, butterflied
  • • 1/2 cup flour
  • • 6 tbsp butter
  • • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • • 1 cup white wine
  • • 2 lemons
  • • 1/2 cup capers
  • • Fresh parsley
  • • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. 1. Dredge chicken in flour
  2. 2. Sauté in butter/oil until golden
  3. 3. Remove chicken, add wine, lemon juice, capers
  4. 4. Return chicken to pan, simmer 5 mins

Roasted Winter Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • • 2 lbs mixed vegetables (brussels sprouts, carrots, butternut squash)
  • • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • • 4 cloves garlic
  • • Fresh rosemary and thyme
  • • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. 1. Cut vegetables uniformly
  2. 2. Toss with oil, herbs, garlic
  3. 3. Roast 400°F for 25-30 mins
