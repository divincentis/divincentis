Notes

  • The key to great gumbo is a properly prepared roux. Don't rush it - the darker the roux, the richer the flavor. But don't burn it!
  • Stock should be warm when added to prevent the roux from seizing.
  • Gumbo can be made ahead through step 5 and refrigerated for up to 3 days.
