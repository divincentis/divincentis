Notes

  • For best results, pound the pork chops to an even thickness to ensure uniform cooking.
  • The oil temperature is crucial - too hot will burn the coating, too cool will make the cutlet greasy.
  • Don't overcook the eggs at the end - they should be soft and slightly runny when served.
  • Traditional dashi stock will give the most authentic flavor, but chicken stock works well as a substitute.
