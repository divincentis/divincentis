Glazed Lemon Cookies 🍋

Prep Time: 20 mins | Chill Time: 45 mins–2 hrs | Bake Time: 14–16 mins | Yield: About 30 cookies

A food processor makes quick work of these bright, buttery cookies. The dough is formed into a log and chilled before slicing and baking. Wrapped in parchment and plastic wrap, the dough keeps in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or in the freezer for up to 2 weeks. The cookies are best eaten the day they are glazed.

Variations

  • Glazed Lemon-Orange Cornmeal Cookies: Substitute 1 tablespoon grated orange zest for an equal amount of lemon zest and 30g (¼ cup) cornmeal for an equal amount of flour.
  • Glazed Lemon and Crystallized Ginger Cookies: Process 3 tablespoons finely chopped crystallized ginger along with the sugar and lemon zest.

Notes

  • You will need a food processor to make these cookies.
  • Dough thickness has been adjusted to ½ inch (from ⅜ inch original).
  • The cookies are best eaten the day they are glazed.
