Instructions

Notes

  • The White BBQ Sauce can be made up to a week in advance and stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
  • For best results, bring chicken to room temperature for 20-30 minutes before grilling.
  • If you don't have a grill, you can use a grill pan or broil in the oven.
  • Pat the chicken extra dry before seasoning to help the rub adhere better.
