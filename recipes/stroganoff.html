Notes

  • If sauce becomes too thick, thin with additional beef stock or pasta water.
  • Cremini mushrooms can be substituted with white button mushrooms.
  • Traditional Stroganoff uses sliced beef instead of ground beef - feel free to substitute with 1 pound of sliced beef sirloin.
  • This is a traditional Russian dish named after the Stroganov family, a prominent aristocratic family in Russia during the 18th and 19th centuries. A French chef working for the Stroganov family is often credited with creating the dish, blending French cooking techniques with Russian ingredients.
